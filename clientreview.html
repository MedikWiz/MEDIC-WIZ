Waste.

Waste refers to any materials, substances, or products that are discarded, no longe useful or unwanted. It can come in various forms, including solid, liquid and gaseous waste, and can be generated from household, industries, agriculture and other human activities. Proper waste management is essential to minimize environmental impact, reduce pollution and conserve resources. Recycling, reusing, and reducing waste are key practices in sustainable living, aiming to create a circular economy where materials are reused rather than discarded.
